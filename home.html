Code Club

Technology has revolutionized the world and holds the key to our future.Coding Club of Sri Sairam Engineering, organize various technical activities at regular intervals in association with Information technology and Computer Science Departments. Coding Club is a platform where students with different interests such as, coding, App development, Machine Learning, and Web Designing. The club is responsible for conducting programming events for the students of Sri Sairam Engineering College . The club arranges weekly workshops focusing on various activities such as, basic programming, web designing languages, machine learning algorithms, and mobile application development. Programming competitions will be conducted to inculcate a coding culture in the institute.
